How to get your non profit off the ground

1. Find a mission

2. Bring together at least 7 like minded people

3. put together your statute in less than 2 minutes

Be sure to receive your status as non profit by using our generator!!

4. Send your Statute to your local tax authority for pre check

Click here to find your responsible tax office or use our generator which at the end tells you where to send it.

5. Go to a notary (find one in your region) and register your association

Let's GO!
